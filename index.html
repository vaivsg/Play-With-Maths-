Rules & Regulation

Play with Maths! calculation result ranges from -19 to 400. It accepts integer value. During division round off of point one decimat value is allowed. Example: if num=5.69231 then num becomes 5.7; For every calculation you have five chances and marks alloted for every calculation is 5. For every wrong answer -1 will be deducted from score. If you reach maximum attempts i.e. 5 chances. You will lose the match. For every right answer your score will be added to high score. Once your highscore reaches 20 or more. You will win the match. For a new calculation press shuffle button. For a new game press restart.
